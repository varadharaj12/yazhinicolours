About Us

A colour house built on precision and trust

"Every metre of fabric that leaves our unit carries a promise — consistent colour, dependable quality, and a delivery date we never miss."

Led by proprietor Jawahar Kumaran, YAZHINI COLOURS operates out of Nethimedu, Salem — one of South India's textile processing hubs. We specialise in fabric dyeing, textile processing and export dyeing, serving domestic manufacturers and international buyers who need colour they can rely on, batch after batch.

Our team blends traditional dyeing craftsmanship with modern process control — from recipe formulation and lab-dip approval to bulk dyeing, finishing and export-ready packing — so every consignment meets the standard your buyer expects.

Our Mission

To deliver export-grade dyeing and fabric processing with unmatched colour accuracy, reliability and speed — for every order, every time.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What types of fabric do you dye and process?

We handle woven and knitted fabrics across cotton, blends and synthetic constructions, processed for both domestic and export orders.

Can you match a specific shade or lab-dip?

Yes. Send us your reference shade or swatch and our colour matching team will develop and share a lab-dip for approval before bulk dyeing begins.

Do you handle export documentation?

Yes. Processing and packing are done with export shipment requirements in mind, and we coordinate documentation alongside your dispatch schedule.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround depends on fabric type, shade complexity and order volume. Share your requirement through the quote form and we'll confirm a schedule.

How do I request a quote?

Fill out the "Request a Quote" form on this page, WhatsApp us, or call us directly — we typically respond within one business day.
